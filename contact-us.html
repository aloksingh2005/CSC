Frequently Asked Questions

What is CSC Seva Center?

CSC Seva Center is managed and promoted by a group of experienced professionals in the field of information technology, Finance & Taxation, Sales & Marketing with over more than 15 years of experience. We provide services like AEPS, Money Transfer, Micro ATM+mPOS, BBPS, Mobile/DTH Recharge, Insurance Services, Business Registration, and Taxation services.

How can I become a CSC agent?

To become a CSC agent, you need to fill out our registration form and submit the required documents. Once approved, you will be provided with login credentials and training to start offering our services.

What services can I offer as a CSC agent?

As a CSC agent, you can offer various services including AEPS (Aadhaar Enable Payment System), Money Transfer, Micro ATM+mPOS, BBPS (Bharat Bill Payment System), Mobile/DTH Recharge, Insurance Services, Business Registration, and Taxation services.

What are the benefits of joining CSC Seva Center?

Joining CSC Seva Center provides you with a platform to offer multiple services, competitive commission structures, regular training and support, marketing materials, and the opportunity to grow your own business while serving your community.

How can I get technical support?

You can reach our technical support team via phone at +91 9990996403 or email at help@cscsevacenter.org. Our support team is available from Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM.
