How to Register

Follow these simple steps to complete your registration

Step 1: Personal Information

Provide your basic details like name, email, phone number, and date of birth to help us identify you.

Step 2: Document Upload

Upload clear scanned copies of your identification documents. Make sure all information is clearly visible.

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport Size Photo

Step 3: Business Details

Tell us about your business or the services you intend to provide through our platform.

Step 4: Verification & Approval

Once submitted, our team will verify your details and documents. You'll receive a confirmation email with next steps.

For any assistance during registration, please contact our support team at +91 9990996403 or email help@cscsevacenter.org
