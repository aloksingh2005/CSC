What is CSC Seva Center?

CSC Seva Center is managed and promoted by a group of experienced professionals in the field of information technology, Finance & Taxation, Sales & Marketing with over 15 years of experience.

We deal in a B2B, B2C model to provide services like AEPS (AADHAR Enable Payment System), Money Transfer, Micro ATM+mPOS, BBPS (Bharat Bill Payment System), Mobile/DTH Recharge, Insurance Services, Business Registration, Taxation services and many more.
